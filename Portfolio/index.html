┣ About Me ┫

Hello! My name is Cameron Grieselding and I am currently a web development student at Eleven Fifty Academy (EFA), a nonprofit code and cybersecurity school.
Prior to pursuing my coding career, I worked at the University of Michigan Hospital as a medical assistant intermediate for five and a half years. In this role I supported over 90 healthcare providers and worked closely with nursing, advanced practice, and physicians in the cardiology, aortic disease, neurology, vascular surgery, and cardiac surgery departments. My favorite part of this position was conducting our cardiac surgery pre-operative teachings, which included rigorous attention to detail.
Having the unique opportunity to work in a world-renowned hospital has provided me with a multitude of experiences that have encouraged me to grow as an individual and as an employee. However, upon moving to Indianapolis, IN my career interests began to shift to the technology field. I've always really enjoyed creating things from nothing, problem-solving when issues arise, and then being able to admire my work in the end. After careful deliberation and plenty of research, I found myself enrolling at EFA and becoming eager to learn something new.
When I'm not busy working in Visual Studio Code, I love running (I'm currently training for a half marathon), reading, gaming, traveling, wakesurfing, snowboarding, and exploring new restaurants.
